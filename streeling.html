Welcome to Streeling University

The craddle of psychohistory

STREELING UNIVERSITY — Is an institution of higher learning in the Streeling Sector of ancient Trantor ... Despite all these claims to fame in the fields of the humanities and sciences alike, it is not for those that the University looms large in today's consciousness. It would probably have come as a total surprise to the generations of scholars at the University to know that in later times Streeling University would be most remembered because a certain Hari Seldon, during the period of The Flight, had been in residence there for a short time.

Dr. Hari Seldon taught at Streeling University as professor of Probability Theory, specifically the area of Psychohistory, a new form of science that he devised. He was head of the department of mathematics
