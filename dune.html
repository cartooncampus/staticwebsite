The Universe of DUNE

ARRAKIS

Discover the Desert That Shapes the Universe

Beyond the great trade routes of the Imperium lies Arrakis—the legendary desert world known across the galaxy as Dune. Harsh, beautiful, and unlike any planet ever settled by humankind, Arrakis is the sole source of the spice melange, the most valuable substance in existence. Here, beneath endless skies of gold and copper, travelers encounter a world of immense scale, ancient mystery, and profound transformation. To visit Arrakis is not merely to journey across a planet, but to stand at the center of history itself.

The cities of Arrakeen and Carthag welcome visitors with a striking fusion of imperial grandeur and desert practicality. Massive wind-carved fortresses rise from the sands, their shaded courtyards and narrow passages designed to endure the relentless heat of the deep desert. Bustling markets offer rare textiles, stillsuit craftsmanship, spice-infused cuisine, and artifacts reflecting centuries of Fremen and off-world influence. By night, the desert air cools beneath dazzling stars unlike any seen elsewhere in the Imperium, while musicians, storytellers, and spice merchants gather beneath lantern-lit terraces overlooking the dunes.

No experience rivals venturing beyond the Shield Wall into the open erg. Guided expeditions allow visitors to witness the awe-inspiring immensity of the desert, where rolling dunes stretch beyond the horizon and colossal sandworms move beneath the sands with terrifying majesty. Travelers may learn the ancient survival traditions of the Fremen, explore hidden sietches carved into rock formations, and discover the remarkable ingenuity of a people who transformed hardship into culture, discipline, and spiritual identity. Every grain of sand on Arrakis carries stories of prophecy, revolution, and survival.

Yet Arrakis offers more than spectacle. It is a world that changes those who walk upon it. Here, visitors confront the delicate relationship between humanity, ecology, and power more vividly than anywhere else in the known universe. The planet’s ambitious terraforming projects, sacred water traditions, and deep ecological mysteries continue to fascinate scientists, pilgrims, and philosophers alike. Whether drawn by commerce, adventure, or destiny, those who come to Arrakis soon understand the ancient Fremen saying: the desert does not merely test people—it reveals them.
